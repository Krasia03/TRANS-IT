The Challenge

The Problem with Traditional Logistics in Tanzania

The logistics and trucking industry in Tanzania remains fragmented and largely informal.

Cargo owners struggle to find reliable trucks quickly

Freight pricing is inconsistent and manually negotiated

Limited visibility once goods are in transit

Truck owners face idle time and unpredictable income

Payment disputes delay cash flow for transporters

Trans-IT modernizes cargo transport in Tanzania by digitizing the entire freight booking workflow — from truck matching to proof of delivery and automated payout.
